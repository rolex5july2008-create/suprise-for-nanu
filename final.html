Just one last thing

I made this knowing there will be days when we’re busy, tired, or a little far from each other. And I wanted you to have something that feels familiar on those days. Something that doesn’t demand anything from you.

I’m really grateful for how naturally we found each other. For the calls that stretched late into the night. For the way conversations felt easy, even when we weren’t saying much.

This isn’t meant to be big or dramatic. It’s just a quiet reminder that you matter to me in simple, steady ways. The kind that don’t disappear when things get quiet.

So whenever you come back to this, whether you’re smiling or just resting, I hope it feels soft. Like something warm you can lean into for a moment.

I’m here. Gently. And always choosing you.
